Post-Silicon Steps in VLSI Design

Understanding the Process After Chip Fabrication-by TANVI BHANDARI-Scientist-B

1. Wafer Testing

Each fabricated wafer undergoes electrical testing to identify functional and defective dies before packaging.

2. Packaging

The verified dies are packaged to protect the chip and provide electrical connections to external systems.

3. Post-Silicon Validation

Chips are tested in real hardware environments to validate performance, functionality, and reliability.

4. Failure Analysis

Any defects found during validation are analyzed to improve yield and future chip revisions.

5. Product Qualification

The final chips undergo reliability and stress testing before being released to the market.
